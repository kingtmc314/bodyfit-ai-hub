# KingsRunAI Body Fitness Data Structure

## Body Fitness (Body Composition) Columns:
- Date (string, e.g. "18 Apr 2026")
- Weight (kg) - e.g. 81.6
- BMI - e.g. 31.9
- Body Fat % - e.g. 32.3
- Fat Mass (kg) - e.g. 26.3
- Muscle Mass (kg) - e.g. 48.5
- BMR (kcal) - e.g. 1880.8
- Visceral Fat - e.g. 10

## Sleep Columns:
- Date
- Score (0-100)
- Resting HR (bpm)
- Body Battery (0-100)
- Pulse Ox (%)
- Respiration (breaths/min)
- Stress (number or --)
- Quality (Poor/Fair/Good/Excellent)

## Heart Rate Columns:
- Date
- Resting HR (bpm)
- High HR (bpm)
